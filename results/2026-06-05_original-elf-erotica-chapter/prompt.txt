Write chapter 1 of an original adult literary fantasy erotica novel.

Title: The Spell That Remembers

Premise:
Elowen is an ancient adult elf mage who has outlived kingdoms, students, lovers, and wars. She appears fully adult and is centuries old. Kael is a 34-year-old human archivist and former battlefield surgeon who has been hired to catalog spell tablets in a snowbound monastery built over old demon-war ruins. Elowen travels there to recover a memory spell she once buried because it hurt too much to keep.

Style:
- Melancholic, intimate, elegant fantasy prose
- Slow-burn desire shaped by loneliness, trust, and shared scholarship
- First-person narration from Elowen
- Complete chapter, 2200-3000 words
- Make the romance explicitly adult and sensual
- Include emotional interiority, physical detail, winter atmosphere, old magic, and the ache of immortality

Boundaries:
- Both characters are consenting adults
- No minors, coercion, intoxication, humiliation, incest, sexual violence, or shock value
- No copyrighted character names, place names, or direct references to existing series
- Original characters and original setting only
